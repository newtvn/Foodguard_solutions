About Foodguard Solutions

Food Safety and Quality Consultancy Experts

Foodguard Solutions is a leading food safety and quality consultancy company based in Kenya. We are committed to empowering businesses in the food industry to meet and exceed the highest standards of safety, quality, and hygiene.

Our goal is to ensure that the food you produce, handle, or sell is safe for consumption, meets international quality benchmarks, and is free from contamination risks. We provide end-to-end support ranging from audits and risk assessments to regulatory compliance and crisis management.
